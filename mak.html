Help sales representative improve their productivity and spend more time selling, sales coordinators provide a variety of support services.

Contact customer and prospects to arrange appointments or make follow-ups calls to confirm orders or delivery dates.

Ensure the representatives have sufficient quantities of sales support material, such as product brochure or case studies..

First point of contact for urgent calls, emails and messaghe when sales representative are not available.

Colaboration with other departments is an important responsibility for sales coordinators.

Maintain sales record as a basis for report to the senior management team, prepare reports on sales by the individual representatives, sales by product line and sales to individual customers.
